# Frid-AI-ys: Executive Summary

**Proposal:** Weekly AI activation initiative to operationalize Tenet 4
**Investment:** Minimal incremental cost (uses existing infrastructure)
**Timeline:** 3-month pilot with defined success criteria
**Natural Owner:** AI Operations (Mike Behl)

---

## The Opportunity

BigCommerce has built excellent AI infrastructure:
- **ChatGPT Enterprise & Gemini** — approved and deployed
- **Knowledge Hubs** — documentation for each tool
- **Prompt Library** — reusable prompts by function
- **AI Governance** — clear data classification and policies
- **Training & Education** — Maria's content and learning modules

**What's missing:** The activation ritual that turns static resources into living practice.

We've articulated **Tenet 4: "Upskill, empower, and enable at scale."** Frid-AI-ys is Tenet 4 operationalized.

---

## The Proposal: Frid-AI-ys

A **45-minute to 2-hour weekly session** dedicated to hands-on AI experimentation using BigCommerce's approved tools and governance framework.

### Rotating Format

| Week | Format | Time | Purpose |
|------|--------|------|---------|
| 1 | Show & Tell | 45 min | Celebrate wins, grow Prompt Library |
| 2 | Prompt Battle | 45 min | Gamified learning, cross-functional teams |
| 3 | Deep Dive Lab | 60 min | Structured skill-building with Knowledge Hub content |
| 4 | The Build | 2-3 hrs | Solve real business problems, generate GPT/workflow requests |

---

## How It Activates Existing Infrastructure

| BigCommerce Asset | How Frid-AI-ys Activates It |
|-------------------|----------------------------|
| **ChatGPT/Gemini Knowledge Hubs** | Deep Dive Labs use Hub content as curriculum |
| **Prompt Library** | Every session contributes entries; Prompt Battles surface best techniques |
| **AI Governance Policies** | Reinforced every session; data classification reviewed in Week 1 |
| **Training & Education** | Frid-AI-ys becomes the "lab" complement to Maria's training |
| **AI Ops Request Process** | Build sessions generate GPT/workflow requests through official intake |
| **Rovo AI** | Integrated for knowledge discovery during sessions |

- BigCommerce already has ChatGPT Enterprise and Gemini deployed
- Knowledge Hubs and Prompt Library already exist
- Governance framework is established

---

## Success Metrics

### Pilot Phase Targets (3 Months)

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Average attendance | 15+ | Sustainable engagement |
| Unique participants | 40+ | Broad reach |
| Functions represented | 80%+ | Cross-functional adoption |
| Prompt Library contributions | 25+ | Knowledge capture and reuse |
| Documented time savings | 100+ hrs | ROI evidence |
| Volunteer facilitators | 5+ | Self-sustaining potential |
| GPT/workflow requests submitted | 5+ | Pipeline for AI Ops |

### Go/No-Go Decision Criteria

**Continue and Scale if:**
- Attendance stable or growing
- 2+ solutions operationalized or in AI Ops pipeline
- Cross-functional participation achieved
- Employee feedback positive (NPS > 30)

**Discontinue or Restructure if:**
- Attendance declining after Month 2
- Zero operationalized outcomes or AI Ops requests
- Single-function participation only
- Negative employee feedback

## The Ask

### Immediate (Pilot Launch)

1. **Protected time:** Approve Friday afternoon sessions (2-3 hours)
2. **Budget:** $150-800/month for materials and prizes
3. **Sponsorship:** Executive endorsement that participation is valued
4. **Ownership:** AI Operations (Mike Behl's team) owns the initiative

### Proposed Pilot Structure

- **Owner:** AI Operations
- **Pilot Group:** Product Engineering (willing to go first)
- **Facilitator:** AI Ops team member or designated champion
- **Executive Sponsor:** SVP Rev Ops and Intelligence

---

## Why AI Operations Should Own This

| Reason | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Fits existing mission** | AI Ops already does "team enablement" |
| **Leverages existing assets** | Knowledge Hubs, Prompt Library, intake process |
| **Scales their impact** | Multiplies reach without 1:1 consulting |
| **Generates pipeline** | Build sessions create GPT/workflow requests |
| **Closes the loop** | Training → Practice → Adoption measurable |

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| "Feels like playtime" | Build sessions must produce AI Ops requests or documented outcomes |
| Governance violations | BigCommerce data classification reviewed every session; approved tools only |
| Single-function attendance | Cross-functional team formation; explicit outreach |
| Facilitator burnout | Build volunteer pipeline; rotate facilitation after Month 2 |
| Competes with AI Ops priorities | AI Ops owns it — sessions feed their pipeline, not distract from it |

---

## Recommendation

**Approve 3-month pilot** with AI Operations as owner and Product Engineering as pilot group.

This isn't a new initiative competing for resources — it's the **activation layer** for investments BigCommerce has already made in AI infrastructure. The upside is accelerated adoption of tools and governance we've already deployed. The downside is bounded: if it doesn't work, we've learned something valuable about organizational readiness.
