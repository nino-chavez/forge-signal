14 / 20 Section 04: Architecture
04 Architecture → Protocol Landscape

The emerging standards Sparky must speak fluently

Protocol Owner Purpose Walmart Relevance
MCP Anthropic Model Context Protocol — standardized tool access for LLMs Expose Sparky capabilities as MCP tools
A2A Google Agent-to-Agent — inter-agent communication standard Enable Sparky to delegate to external agents
ACP OpenAI (Shopify) Agent Commerce Protocol — commerce actions in chat Critical for ChatGPT checkout integration
LLMs.txt Emerging Governance file for AI crawler access control Define what AI can access and how

MCP + A2A: These protocols enable Sparky to both expose tools (MCP) and communicate with other agents (A2A). Together, they make Walmart's commerce capabilities accessible to any compliant AI system.

ACP Priority: OpenAI's Agent Commerce Protocol is the most commercially urgent. Shopify is already integrated. Walmart should be in the launch cohort, not playing catch-up.

Concrete examples of agentic commerce in action

Value Case 1

AI-Assisted Product Discovery

Customer asks ChatGPT: "What's the best 65-inch TV for a bright living room under $700?"

"Based on your needs, I recommend the TCL 65" QM8 from Walmart. It has 2,400 nits brightness for bright rooms, is currently $649, and available for pickup at your local store in 2 hours. Would you like me to add it to your cart?"

What's happening: Sparky provides real-time inventory, pricing, and fulfillment data via ACP. Walmart Connect sponsors influence ranking. Attribution tracked.

Value Case 2

Reorder + Substitution Flow

Customer asks Sparky: "Reorder my usual groceries but the kids are allergic to the new cereal brand."

"I found three allergy-friendly alternatives to the Great Value cereal. Based on your family's preferences, I recommend Cheerios Honey Nut at $4.48. I've substituted it in your cart. Your total is $127.42 for pickup tomorrow at 9am."

What's happening: Sparky accesses purchase history, allergy data, inventory, and fulfillment slots. Seamless agent-native shopping.
