The Opportunity Gap

Shopify captures 48% of migrations by default—but many merchants would prefer to keep WordPress if they could upgrade just the commerce layer.

Commerce.com currently captures ~8% with no WordPress-specific positioning.

Phase 0 validates whether "Keep WordPress, Upgrade Commerce" can shift this share.

Source: Cart2Cart Migration Facts & Statistics 2024 (shopping-cart-migration.com)
The Insight

They're not leaving WordPress.
They're leaving WooCommerce.

What merchants love about WordPress:

  • Content management excellence
  • SEO-rich blog content (often 500+ posts)
  • Familiar editing experience
  • Existing team expertise & investment

What fails in WooCommerce:

  • Database architecture (wp_postmeta EAV model)
  • Checkout performance & conversion
  • Plugin dependency hell
  • PCI compliance burden (self-hosted)
Hypothesis: A meaningful segment would choose WordPress + BigCommerce over Shopify if the option existed.
