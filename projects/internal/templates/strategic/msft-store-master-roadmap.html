Master Strategic Roadmap - Design Rationale

Structure Decisions

  • 6 capability swimlanes instead of 12+ platform rows - executives see domains, not tools
  • 4 value-oriented phases (Mobilize → Foundation → Scale → Optimize) instead of "Plan & Analyze → Build"
  • Platform names in subtext - visible but not the primary organizing principle
  • Quarterly time scale - appropriate for strategic view (not monthly for this audience)
