## The Philosophy

### Who This Is For

**This path is for you if:**

- You want a map of the territory I've actually crossed
- You're willing to build real things that force real learning
- You want to prove to yourself—not anyone else—that you're serious
- You understand that reps can't be shortcut, but wrong reps can be avoided

**This path is NOT for you if:**

- You want a curriculum to passively consume
- You need someone to tell you exactly what to do next
- You're collecting credentials for LinkedIn

I didn't learn this from a course. I learned it by:

1. **Starting with fear** — of being left behind
2. **Building something real** — not a tutorial project

You can't shortcut the reps. But you can avoid wasting time on the wrong reps.
