## Detailed Findings

### SECTION 1: Market Size & Growth Statistics

#### Claim: AI Agents Market 2025 = $7.6B
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED (with minor variance)
- **Sources:**
  - [Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/ai-agents-market-report): $7.60B (2025)
  - [GM Insights](https://www.gminsights.com/industry-analysis/ai-agents-market): $7.7B (2025)
  - [MarketsandMarkets](https://www.marketsandmarkets.com/Market-Reports/ai-agents-market-15761548.html): $7.84B (2025)
- **Note:** Figures range from $7.6B-$7.84B depending on source. $7.6B is accurate.

#### Claim: Projected 2030 = $50B
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Sources:**
  - [Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/ai-agents-market-report): $50.31B by 2030
  - [MarketsandMarkets](https://www.marketsandmarkets.com/Market-Reports/ai-agents-market-15761548.html): $52.62B by 2030
- **Note:** Both major research firms confirm ~$50B projection.

#### Claim: CAGR = 45.8%
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Sources:**
  - [Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/ai-agents-market-report): 45.8% CAGR (2025-2030)
  - [MarketsandMarkets](https://www.marketsandmarkets.com/Market-Reports/ai-agents-market-15761548.html): 46.3% CAGR
  - [Mordor Intelligence](https://www.mordorintelligence.com/industry-reports/agentic-ai-market): 43.61% CAGR
- **Note:** 45.8% is the Grand View Research figure; other sources show 43-46% range.

#### Claim: Enterprise Adoption 2025 = 85%
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Sources:**
  - [DemandSage](https://www.demandsage.com/ai-agents-statistics/): "85% of organizations have integrated AI agents in at least one workflow"
  - [Index.dev](https://www.index.dev/blog/ai-agents-statistics): Confirms 85% figure
- **Note:** This refers to AI agents specifically, not general AI adoption.

#### Claim: Gartner - 33% of enterprise software will include agentic AI by 2028
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [Gartner Press Release (June 2025)](https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025-06-25-gartner-predicts-over-40-percent-of-agentic-ai-projects-will-be-canceled-by-end-of-2027)
- **Exact Quote:** "33% of enterprise software applications will include agentic AI by 2028, up from less than 1% in 2024"

#### Claim: 90% of non-tech companies plan to put agents in production
- **Status:** ⚠️ NEEDS CORRECTION
- **Finding:** Could not verify this exact figure from LangChain State of AI Agents report.
- **Recommendation:** Remove or replace with: "According to a Capgemini survey, more than half of large enterprises plan to use AI agents in the next year, and 82% will integrate them within three years."

### SECTION 2: Talent Gap & Skills Statistics

#### Claim: Expected AI Talent Gap = 50% (Reuters)
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Sources:**
  - [IBM AI Skills Gap](https://www.ibm.com/think/insights/ai-skills-gap): Cites Reuters as source
  - [InformationWeek](https://www.informationweek.com/it-leadership/the-ai-skills-gap-and-how-to-address-it): Confirms Reuters 50% figure
- **Original Source:** Reuters article "Needed AI skills facing unknown regulations and advancements" (December 6, 2023)

#### Claim: LinkedIn - Job Postings 78% YoY growth, Talent Pool 24% growth
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [Second Talent](https://www.secondtalent.com/resources/global-ai-talent-shortage-statistics/): "LinkedIn Global Talent Insights reveals that AI job postings have increased 78% year-over-year, while the talent pool has grown only 24%"
- **Note:** Data from Q4 2024 - Q1 2025 collection period.

#### Claim: LLM Skills Demand Increase = 340% since 2023
- **Status:** ⚠️ NEEDS CORRECTION
- **Finding:** This figure is cited by [Second Talent](https://www.secondtalent.com/resources/global-ai-talent-shortage-statistics/) as coming from Coursera Global Skills Report 2024, but could not be independently verified in Coursera's public reports.
- **Coursera's Actual Data:**
  - 1,060% YoY increase in GenAI course enrollments (2023-2024)
  - 195% YoY increase in GenAI course enrollments (2024-2025)
- **Recommendation:** Replace with: "GenAI course enrollments increased over 1,000% YoY (Coursera Global Skills Report 2024)" or remove the 340% claim.

#### Claim: 40% of workforce will need reskilling (executive estimate)
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** Multiple sources cite this as an executive survey finding
- **Related:** [McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai) cites similar workforce transformation estimates

#### Claim: 51% of organizations lack skilled AI talent in-house
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [IBM AI Skills Gap](https://www.ibm.com/think/insights/ai-skills-gap): "More than half (51%) acknowledge they don't have the right mix of skilled AI talent in-house"

#### Claim: 46% cite skill gaps as #1 barrier to AI success
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [McKinsey State of AI 2025](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai): "The most prominent reason cited for barriers to AI success is talent skill gaps, accounting for 46% of responses"

#### Claim: 78% of execs say AI advances too fast for training
- **Status:** ❓ UNVERIFIABLE
- **Finding:** This specific statistic could not be verified from Accenture's research.
- **Recommendation:** Replace with verified Accenture finding: "82% of companies at the early stage of operations maturity have not applied a talent reinvention strategy" (Accenture)

### SECTION 5: Salary & ROI Data

#### Claim: AI Engineer average salary = $206K (Glassdoor)
- **Status:** ⚠️ NEEDS CORRECTION
- **Finding:** [Glassdoor (December 2025)](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/ai-engineer-salary-SRCH_KO0,11.htm) shows:
  - AI Engineer: $138,894/year average
  - Artificial Intelligence Engineer: $148,619/year average
  - Senior AI Engineer: $283,745/year average
- **Correction:** The $206K figure may have been for a specific subset or location. National average is ~$139-149K.
- **Recommendation:** Update to: "AI Engineer average salary $139K-$149K; Senior AI Engineers up to $284K (Glassdoor, December 2025)"

#### Claim: Prompt Engineer average salary = $136K (Glassdoor)
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [Glassdoor (February 2025)](https://www.glassdoor.com/Salaries/prompt-engineer-salary-SRCH_KO0,15.htm): "$136,141 as of February 2025"
- **Updated Data:** December 2025 shows $126,135-$137,609 depending on title variation
- **Note:** Figure is accurate for the time cited; current data shows slight variance.

#### Claim: GenAI delivers $3.70 ROI per dollar invested (McKinsey)
- **Status:** ⚠️ NEEDS CORRECTION - MISATTRIBUTED
- **Finding:** This statistic is from **Microsoft**, not McKinsey.
- **Source:** [Microsoft Blog](https://opentools.ai/news/microsoft-reports-a-staggering-37x-roi-with-generative-aiheres-how): "businesses are achieving an impressive $3.70 return for every $1 invested in generative AI"
- **Correction:** Change attribution from "McKinsey" to "Microsoft" or remove McKinsey reference.

#### Claim: AI consulting market $72.8B by 2030, 31.6% CAGR
- **Status:** ✅ VERIFIED
- **Source:** [GlobeNewswire (November 2025)](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2025/11/21/3192566/0/en/AI-Consulting-and-Support-Services-Market-Analysis-Report-2025-2032-Adoption-Surges-as-70-of-Businesses-Accelerate-Integration-Across-Healthcare-Finance-Retail-and-Manufacturing.html): "valued at US$14 billion in 2024, is forecast to expand at a CAGR of 31.6% during 2024-2030 to reach US$72.8 billion by 2030"
