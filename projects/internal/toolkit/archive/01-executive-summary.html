What Is This?

A reference architecture for enabling AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) to shop, transact, and get support on behalf of customers through your commerce platform.

B2C Retail AI-Native Vendor-Neutral

Why Now?

  • ● Google AP2 enabling agent payments
  • ● Anthropic MCP becoming standard for tool use
  • ● Early movers capturing agent-driven traffic
