Governance Model: Global vs. Local

Strictly enforce what is centralized (80%) vs. flexible (20%) to maintain scale

Global Core (80%) - Non-Negotiable
1 Security Framework - Unified threat management
2 Checkout Logic - Consistent conversion optimization
3 Core Integrations - Single hub to maintain
4 Design System - Brand consistency, accessibility
5 Identity/SSO - Microsoft account integration
6 Analytics - Unified reporting and insights
Local Flexibility (20%) - Per-Market
1 Content/Translation - Local teams own messaging
2 Regional Promotions - Market-specific campaigns
3 Local Payment Methods - Pix, iDEAL, etc.
4 Local Shipping - Regional carrier integrations
5 Tax/Compliance - Jurisdiction-specific rules
6 Local Promotions - Regional marketing autonomy
