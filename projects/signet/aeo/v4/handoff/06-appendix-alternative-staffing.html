Appendix: Alternative Staffing Model

Version: 1.0 | Last Updated: December 2024

Context & Rationale

The baseline staffing model assumes the Lead Architect possesses AWS CDK (Cloud Development Kit) expertise to implement infrastructure-as-code deliverables. If a Lead Architect candidate cannot be identified with this specific skill, an alternative staffing model can offload CDK development to an offshore infrastructure engineer.

When to Use This Model:
  • Lead Architect candidates lack CDK/IaC experience
  • AI Engineer is not versed in CDK (confirmed assumption)
  • Cost optimization is a priority
  • Offshore resources with CDK skills are available

D5: CDK Infrastructure Skeleton (16 hours)

  1. CDK App Structure — lib/, bin/, test/ directories
  2. Environment Configs — dev, staging, prod parameter files
  3. S3 Buckets — raw-data, reports, manual-uploads with lifecycle policies

  6. EventBridge Schedule — Distillation trigger (Sunday 2am UTC)

Recommendation

Recommendation: Adopt Alternative Staffing Model

If a Lead Architect with CDK expertise cannot be identified within the staffing timeline, the alternative model provides:

  • Staffing flexibility — Broader candidate pool for Lead Architect
  • Cost savings — ~$5,600 reduction in professional services fees
  • Specialized skills — Dedicated CDK expertise vs. generalist approach
  • Manageable risk — Clear coordination model with built-in oversight

Decision Criteria

Scenario Recommended Model
Lead Architect candidate has CDK experience Baseline Model (simpler coordination)
Lead Architect candidate lacks CDK experience Alternative Model (offshore CDK engineer)
Cost optimization is priority Alternative Model (~$5.6K savings)
Minimal coordination overhead is priority Baseline Model (single owner)
Offshore resources unavailable or restricted Baseline Model (must find CDK-skilled Lead Arch)

Next Steps (If Adopting Alternative Model)

  1. Update SOW — Add offshore CDK Engineer role with 40-hour budget
  2. Revise Lead Architect hours — Reduce from 284 to 252 hours
  3. Identify offshore resource — Begin sourcing Week -2 (before kickoff)
  4. Prepare CDK requirements doc — Lead Architect drafts Week 1, Day 1
  5. Establish communication channels — Slack channel, GitHub repo access
