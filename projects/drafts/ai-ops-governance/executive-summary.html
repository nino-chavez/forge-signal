AI Operations Governance Framework

Executive Summary for Leadership Alignment

Situation

Commerce.com has approved AI tools with selective usage policies, data classification (4-tier), and documented prompt libraries. Individual productivity gains are occurring, but adoption is uneven and workflows are not standardized.

Complication

Current policies address chat-based AI usage but leave gaps for agentic workflows (autonomous tool chains), multi-model orchestration, and output quality governance. Advanced practitioners are constrained; less experienced users lack clear paths to productivity. Production outpaces consumption—no clear gates distinguish draft from shippable.

Resolution

Establish a cross-functional governance model that extends existing policies for agentic workflows, defines quality gates for AI-assisted output, and creates a maturity path from individual experimentation to scaled, compliant operations.
