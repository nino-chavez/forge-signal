AI Operations Maturity Assessment

Self-Assessment Survey for Product Teams

Instructions: Answer each question based on your current, honest assessment. This is not a test—it helps us understand where the team is today so we can plan appropriate enablement. Select the option that best describes your typical behavior.

Tool Familiarity
Question 1
How often do you use AI assistants (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, etc.) for work tasks?
Question 2
How many different AI tools have you used for work in the past 3 months?
Prompt Craft
Question 3
How would you describe your typical prompts?
Question 4
When an AI response isn't quite right, what do you typically do?
Output Judgment
Question 5
How do you handle AI-generated output before using it?
Question 6
How confident are you in identifying when AI output is wrong or hallucinated?
Security Awareness
Question 7
How do you decide what information is safe to share with AI tools?
Question 8
Are you aware of the company's AI usage policies?
Workflow Integration
Question 9
How is AI integrated into your development or work environment?
Question 10
Do you have reusable prompts, templates, or custom GPTs for common tasks?

Scoring Guide

Score Range Maturity Level Description
0-5 Level 1: Experimental Limited or no AI usage. Opportunity for basic enablement.
6-12 Level 2: Sanctioned Using approved tools but individually. Room for workflow development.
13-20 Level 3: Practiced Regular usage with good practices. Ready for advanced patterns.
21-26 Level 4: Integrated AI embedded in workflow with quality gates. Potential champion.
27-30 Level 5: Agentic Advanced usage with automation. Can help enable others.

Note: This assessment is for planning purposes only. Aggregate results help identify enablement priorities and champion candidates. Individual responses are not used for performance evaluation.
